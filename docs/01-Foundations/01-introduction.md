# Chapter 1: Introduction to AI

## 1.1 What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a broad field of computer science that gives computers the ability to perform human-like cognitive functions such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

### 1.1.1 Historical Overview

The concept of AI has been around for decades, with its roots in ancient myths and philosophical discussions about artificial beings. The modern field of AI was founded in 1956 at a workshop at Dartmouth College.

## 1.2 Why Study AI?

AI is transforming industries, creating new opportunities, and addressing complex global challenges. Understanding AI is becoming increasingly crucial for various professionals.

### 1.2.1 Impact on Society

AI's impact spans across healthcare, finance, transportation, and entertainment, enhancing efficiency and driving innovation.
