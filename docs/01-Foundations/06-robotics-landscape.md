# Chapter 6: The Robotics Landscape

## 6.1 Introduction to Robotics

Robotics is an interdisciplinary field that integrates computer science, engineering, and AI to design, construct, operate, and apply robots. Robots are machines that can automate tasks and interact with the physical world.

### 6.1.1 Types of Robots

Robots come in various forms, including industrial robots for manufacturing, service robots for domestic or commercial tasks, and mobile robots for exploration and navigation.

## 6.2 Robotic Systems and Applications

Modern robotics spans a wide array of applications, leveraging advanced AI techniques to perform complex tasks in diverse environments.

### 6.2.1 Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

AMRs are intelligent robots that can navigate and operate in unstructured environments without direct human supervision, commonly used in logistics, healthcare, and exploration.
